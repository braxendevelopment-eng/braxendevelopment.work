Quarter Club

Braxen Development’s Quarter Club is designed to help businesses rebuild credit reputation. The lowest subscription starts at just $0.25 per month, giving you access to the essentials of establishing financial credibility.

For $10, you can also gain the Path to Profit Handbook, a practical guide that provides insight into managing business finances. Additional solutions are available, including consultation packages for business development needs, which come with a signed NDA.

Subscription Tiers

  • $0.25/mo – Founders’ Circle (Annual)
  • $1/mo – Dollar Bill (Annual)
  • $2/mo – Bread & Butter (Annual)
  • $5/mo – Startup Lane (Monthly)
  • $10/mo – Builder’s Foundation (Monthly)
  • $20/mo – Growth Track (Monthly)
  • $50/mo – Pro Business (Monthly)
  • $100/mo – Executive Class (Monthly)

Note: Only tiers $5/mo and higher allow monthly subscriptions; the first three are annual only. Multiple annual tiers can be purchased simultaneously.
